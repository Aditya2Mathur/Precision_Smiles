Our Mission

Your Health, Our Priority

Precision Smiles is a modern dental clinic located inside Jain Hospital, Azizganj, Shahjahanpur. This hospital is home to experienced doctors, including a General Physician, Gynecologist, and Pediatrician, making it a trusted place for complete family healthcare.

Founded by Dr. Puneet Jain (B.D.S. & M.D.S), Precision Smiles was started with one goal—to provide comfortable, pain-free, and advanced dental care to people of all ages. With 10+ years of experience, Dr. Jain is known for his gentle treatment and care in areas like braces, dental implants, laser dentistry, teeth alignment, jaw correction, and smile makeovers.

Many families looking for a dentist near me, orthodontist, or pediatric dentist near me have trusted Precision Smiles for honest advice and quality results. With the latest tools like laser treatment and digital OPG, our clinic makes dental visits simple, safe, and stress-free.
